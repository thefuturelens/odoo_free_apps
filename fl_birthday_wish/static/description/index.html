Birthday Wish

This module will wish the employees, customers and partners on their birthday

Employee

Go to Employees > Employees > Private Information.

Add date of birth for employee.

Add employee work email.

Partner

Go to Contacts > Contacts.

Add date of birth for partner.

Add partner email.

Email Template

Greetings can be sent by the automatic cron scheduler.

Email template for birthday wish.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo Community Edition but not tested in Enterprise Edition.
